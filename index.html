Welcome to AAKAKA Care our goal is to make your life better

AAKAKA Home Care is a domiciliary care agency that provides high standard of person-centred home care in Woking, Guildford, Runnymede and other surrounding. We have substantial experience in providing care at home and support in the community. AAKAKA Care works with adults with physical disabilities, learning disabilities and mental health conditions. We at AAKAKA, think about care and support in an entirely different way. We focus on personalisation, meaning we start with the person as an individual with strengths, preferences and aspirations, and putting them at the centre of identifying their needs and giving them choices about how they want to live their lives. Our Care staff are trained and supported to ensure they uphold our values of Compassion, Choice, dignity and respect. So you can be rest assured that you will receive the highest possible standard of care and support at home.
